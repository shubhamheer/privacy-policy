This Privacy Policy explains how GkWise - SSC Railway Practice ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The only data stored locally on your device is:

  • Bookmarks: Questions you bookmark within the app are saved locally on your device for your convenience. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Firebase Services

Our app uses Google Firebase for backend functionality, including:

  • Firebase Firestore: Used to store and retrieve quiz questions and content. No personal user data is stored in Firestore.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Used to send push notifications to users. FCM may use a device token to deliver notifications. This token does not identify you personally.
  • Firebase Analytics: May collect anonymous usage data such as app opens and screen views to help us improve the app.

3. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you push notifications about new content, updates, or important announcements. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings. No personal data is stored or shared for this purpose.

4. Advertising

Our app displays advertisements through AppLovin MAX. AppLovin and its advertising partners may collect device information such as device ID, IP address, and usage data for the purpose of serving relevant ads. This data is collected and handled according to AppLovin's own privacy policy.

We do not share any personal information with advertisers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, FCM, Analytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • AppLovin MAX (Ads) — applovin.com/privacy

6. No Payment Data

GkWise does not include any in-app purchases or payment features. We do not collect or process any financial information.

7. Children's Privacy

GkWise is an educational app intended for students preparing for SSC, Railway, and other competitive exams. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data within our app. Bookmark data is stored only on your local device and is never transmitted to external servers.
